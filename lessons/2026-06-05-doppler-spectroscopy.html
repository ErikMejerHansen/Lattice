The reflex radial velocity of a Sun-mass star pulled by a planet of mass M_p on a circular, edge-on orbit of period P, plotted over one orbital cycle. The vertical scale auto-fits to the current K amplitude. The three dashed horizontal lines mark the precision floors of three eras of spectrograph: ELODIE 1995 (~13 m/s), HARPS 2003 (~1 m/s), ESPRESSO 2018 (~10 cm/s). When a threshold lies outside the visible y range, an arrow at the canvas edge marks its direction. The detect tag in the readout reports the first instrument era whose floor sits below K.
