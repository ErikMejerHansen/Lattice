Three-level (ruby, Maiman 1960)

Threshold: R = γ (more than half the active ions must be lifted out of the ground state).

Four-level (Nd:YAG, Geusic 1964)

Threshold: any R > 0 (the lower laser level decays to ground at rate Γ much larger than γ and stays empty).

Steady-state populations of two model laser media under pump rate R, given here in units of the spontaneous decay rate γ of the lasing transition. The fast non-radiative decay rate Γ (dashed gray arrow on each diagram) is held at 100γ throughout. Δ is the inversion density N_upper − N_lower; positive Δ means the medium amplifies a beam at the lasing frequency, negative Δ means it absorbs.
