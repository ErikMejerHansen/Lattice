Cross-section of a step-index fiber. The core (n₁ = 1.50, darker center band) is bounded above and below by cladding (n₂, lighter bands). Slide n₂ from 1.400 to 1.495 to vary the index contrast. The translucent green wedge in air on the left is the acceptance cone of half-angle θ_a. A green ray entering well inside the cone refracts at the end face and zig-zags down the core by total internal reflection. An amber ray entering near the cone's edge zig-zags more shallowly. A red ray entering just past the edge refracts in, hits the cladding wall at less than the critical angle θ_c, and escapes into the cladding on its first bounce.
