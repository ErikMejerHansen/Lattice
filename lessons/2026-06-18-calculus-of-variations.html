For the straight-line base, ε = 0 sits at the minimum of J(ε) and the tangent there is flat: dJ/dε vanishes, the line is the optimum of the arc-length functional. Switch to the parabola and the dot is no longer at the bottom of the curve: the tangent tilts, dJ/dε ≠ 0, and the parabola is not the curve nature would pick.
