Planck spectrum on log-wavelength axes. The shaded area under the curve is the integrated total energy density. Drag T: the curve grows and shifts, the area swells. The readout shows the numerical integral side by side with the analytic prediction aT⁴ — they track to four significant figures wherever you push the slider, because the integral of Planck is exactly Stefan's law. The inset on the upper right plots radiated flux against T on log axes, with a moving dot sliding along a line of slope 4.
