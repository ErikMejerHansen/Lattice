The Hertzsprung-Russell plane. Luminosity (in suns) up the side, surface temperature along the bottom and running hot to cool from left to right, both logarithmic. Faint diagonals are lines of constant radius, each a tenfold step, drawn from L = 4πR²σT⁴. Drag the sliders to move the star and read its inferred radius. The constant-radius stripes are exactly what split the diagram into white dwarfs, the main sequence, giants, and supergiants.
